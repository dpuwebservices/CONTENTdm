About

The DePaul University Libraries Digital Collections include a variety of online images for researchers and the general public. The collections include historical photographs of the Lincoln Park neighborhood surrounding DePaul University's main campus, historical photos of DePaul University buildings and Vincentian maps, postcards and other ephemera.
