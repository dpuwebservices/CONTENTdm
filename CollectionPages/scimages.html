Exhibits from the
DePaul University Libraries and Partners

The Exhibits from the DePaul University Libraries and Partners Digital Collection preserves selected related materials from physical exhibits mounted by the DePaul University Libraries. These exhibits showcase and explain the significance of individual objects and their relationships to other materials and the large collections of which they are a part.

The DePaul University Libraries have an active exhibits program that shares, interprets and promotes our collections and services. More information on our exhibits programs and contact information may be found through our Special Collections and Archives department and through our Library Outreach Services department
