DePaul University Photographs

This collection contains digitized photographs offering vibrant documentation of DePaul’s campus culture, including academic and extra-curricular activities. The original photographs, spanning the years 1898 to the present day, have come from individual students, academic departments, and a variety of other campus offices and university publications.

Original photographs are held in DePaul the University Library Special Collections and Archives.
